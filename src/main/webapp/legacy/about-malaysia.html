ABOUT MALAYSIA

Malaysia is a country bordered by Indonesia, Singapore, Thailand, Brunei and Philippine. Its capital is Kuala Lumpur. Malaysia has 13 states are divided into 2 regions. There are many tourist areas in Malaysia, Johor If you visit, you can visit the beautiful Mount Ledang and Hot Water Week (Hot Water Village) which is where hot showers are efficacious to cure various diseases, especially skin diseases. Johor River you will be able to see lightning and not so much as blinking stars in the sky.

Malacca is a state in Malaysia which has many tourist attractions such as the Baba Nyonya Heritage Museum; Famosa is a Portuguese fort built when it landed in Malacca. In Malacca there is also a zoo and you can also create a riverboat trip on the river of Malacca. Redang Island is an island that is very romantic, suitable to be used as a place to honeymoon. There we could scuba diving, wind surfing, fishing, or snorkeling. The island is highly recommended to be a tourist area, because the place is so natural. In fact you will not find a bank or ATM here, the store was difficult to be found. You need to Kuala Terengganu to take the money before a trip to the island of Redang.

For more information you can go to gomalaysiamap.com, Malaysia still has some places that are also interesting to visit such as, Petaling Street which is a China Town in Malaysia, Bukit Bintang Walk is where shopping for the shopaholic, and Central Market is now a tourist center and a place to buy souvenirs by. If you are a fan of chocolate, make sure you come to Cocoa Boutique which is a chocolate shop center, you should try the chocolate content of durian are very famous.

In fact Malaysia does not have islands of Indonesia, ethnicity and culture are still limited and the culture brought from the neighboring countries of Malaysia, but because the Malaysian government has worked hard to build Malaysia to become a tourist area, with the slogan “Truly Asia”, Malaysia became a top 10 countries The best tourist destination in the world.
